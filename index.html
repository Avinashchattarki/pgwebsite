Welcome to Lakshmana Durga Co-living Stays

Your Ideal Place to Stay in BTM Layout, Bengaluru

Lakshmana Durga PG offers an excellent living space at an affordable price in the heart of Bengaluru's BTM Layout. We provide clean and comfortable rooms with a variety of options to choose from, including 3, 4, 5, and 6 sharing accommodations.

Rules & Regulations

  1. Notice Period: If anyone wishes to vacate the PG, they must inform management 30 days in advance. Otherwise, one month’s rent must be paid.
  2. Fee Payment: Fees should be paid by the 1st and 5th of every calendar month, as per your choice.
  3. Documentation: A photocopy of your ID proof (Aadhar card) and a passport-sized photo must be submitted at the time of occupancy.
  4. Non-Refundable Fees: Once paid, the token advance and rent cannot be refunded or transferred.
  5. Guest Policy: Outside visitors are not allowed without management permission.
  6. Guest Accommodation: Accommodation for guests, with or without food, will be charged ₹500 per day.
  7. Loss of Articles: The management is not responsible for the loss of any personal items.
  8. Room Departure: Please ensure that all lights, fans, and geysers are switched off before leaving your room.
  9. Keys: We are not responsible for the loss of room keys or wardrobe keys. If you lose the cupboard key or door key, lock removal charges will be ₹500.
  10. Quietness: Do not talk loudly in front of the PG main gate.
  11. Post-Vacation Charges: ₹500 per day will be charged after the vacating date.
  12. Maintenance Charge: A maintenance charge of ₹1000 is applicable upon vacating.
  13. Prohibited Activities: Smoking, drinking, loud music, and induction stoves are not allowed inside the room.
  14. Restricted Hours: Entry and exit are restricted between 11:00 PM and 5:00 AM. Those with night shifts will be allowed.
  15. Room Assignments: The management reserves the right to change or shift your room without prior notice.
  16. TV and Phone Usage: The TV should be switched off after 10:30 PM. Mobile phones should be used without disturbing your roommate.
  17. Food Policy: There will be no food provided for 4 days yearly (not continuously). Please cooperate with management.
  18. Outside Activities: The management is not responsible for activities that occur outside the PG.
  19. Damage Responsibility: Any damage inside the room or within the PG will be chargeable.
  20. Policy Changes: All rules and regulations are subject to change by management as required.
